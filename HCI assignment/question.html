FAQ

1. How to buy products?

  1. Go to the Products page.
  2. Choose a product and click the "Add To Cart" button.
  3. Click the shopping cart icon to view choosed product and click "Checkout".

2. How to register an account?

  1. Click the Sign Up button at the top of the page.
  2. Input your personal information correctly.
  3. Once you've filled out the form, click the "Create Account" button.

3. When will my ordered items arrive?

Normally, the order processing time takes around 3 to 4 weeks counting from confirmation of purchase order and its settlement of payment, except orders under special programs with special arrangement. A notification email will send to your registered email account once your purchased items are ready to deliver or ready to pick up.
